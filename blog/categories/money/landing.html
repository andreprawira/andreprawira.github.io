Bank

In the states, people typically use 2 type of bank accounts, checking and savings account. The former is a simple account that majority does not accrue any interest but you can use it to deposit and money whenever you want without any restriction. While the latter, is an account that you can deposit money and the bank will give an APY (Annual Percentage Yield)

Credit Cards

In the States, banks offers credit cards that come with benefits. For example, I got a free roundtrip plane ticket to Indonesia with Chase. However, before you open any credit card, you need a social security number. If you just got a social security number, APPLY for a credit card soon as possible.

As a person who lives in the states, legally you are required to file your taxes even though you do not work nor you have a social security number. And I encourage you to do so. But, why? Well, you will get a TAX REFUND!
