First Day

Welcome to the States, whether you are going to study in the beautiful California or the windy Illinois or the cold Minnesota, prepare yourself, your real life has just begun. Things you need to get and do

  • I-20
  • Passport
  • I-90
  • Supplemental documents
  • Notebook, pencil, and pens
  • Calculator
  • Scantron (buat ujian)
  • Update your physical address
  • Open a bank account and get a mobile plan
  • Choose a major
  • Student health insurance
Make sure you know your personal school email and password on the first day. If you dont, you will end up like me, having to read 80+ emails after not checking my email for a week. If you choose to study in a community college then transfer to a university, you will have plenty of spare time to do, make use of those times, study hard, think about what do you want to do to earn money for the rest of your life, do a research on the job, pick the major that will get you there.

Remember, your life has just begun, every choice you take matters. Dont fuck around in the states, urusan sama polisi can get you jailed and or deported. Use your best judgement before doing something . Make friends, join clubs, study hard, get yourself enough sleep, and keep yourself healthy (not gonna lie, being sick in this country can get you broke or actually die or both).
